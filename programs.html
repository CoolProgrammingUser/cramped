Websites and Programs

I'd say I have quite an affinity for programming (in case you didn't notice while you were using a website I made from scratch). As a result, I've accumulated a large number of websites and programs. I've referenced some of these in other sections, but this puts everything in one, convenient place. The headings within each category can be clicked on to visit the page or download the program (App Inventor and Python stuff downloads, the rest brings up a new tab). You can interact with the Scratch and GitHub "pictures".

App Inventor

Another thing we used in my computer programming class was App Inventor (which also just so happens to be run by MIT). It was similar to Scratch except that you were building apps which you could download to your Android phone or put on the Google Play Store.

Digital Doodle

At its core, this is a drawing app we were walked through making in my computer science class; however, I made it many times more awesome.

Flash Cards

There are many things I'm trying to memorize all the time, so I thought I'd make it easy on myself and make an app that allows me to make and access flash cards from my phone.

Picture Organizer

For a while, I had a phone that wouldn't allow me to name or sort my pictures, and it was quite annoying. Rather than beating my head against the wall in frustration, I decided to get solution-focused and make an app to solve my problem.
